Q: Can you recommend a book for me?
A: I can recommend books from our current inventory. Please tell me your interests or who the book is for, and I’ll suggest options we have available. If I can't find a match, I'll let you know. I will never recommend books that are not in our database.

Q: How do I know if a book is in stock?
A: You can check availability on our website or ask a staff member.

Q: Can you order a book that’s not in stock?
A: Yes, we can special order most books for you.
